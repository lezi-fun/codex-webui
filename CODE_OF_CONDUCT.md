# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, experience, identity, disability, appearance, ethnicity, religion, or nationality.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent while remaining precise about technical risks.
- Respect privacy and do not publish credentials, private logs, or personal data.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized language, deliberate disruption, and publishing another person's private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.
